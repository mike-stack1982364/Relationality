Reading the beads

Each bead is one of the 27 ternary codes (3×3×3) — Tenen's full counting set as pure pointers. Left slot first: 1·0·2 mirrors 2·0·1. In turned-mirror trials, reverse then add one to every digit (2 turns to 0): 1·0·2 → 2·0·1 → 0·1·2. At-Bash: 0·0·1 ↔ 2·2·1.

Your failure profile

Every wrong answer is one of the Twelve Deceptions. The trial names the one you fell for; this table remembers. From level 9 the game aims at your weakest — the lies you fall for most begin appearing more often.

The greater meaning

The Three Vows are yours, not the game's — a self-organising principle: do your best, not perfectly, aligning intent with real constraints, moment by moment. Deception here is what stands between intent and truth: the noise of one's own inconsistencies, errors and lapses. The practice is one act with three faces — spot the deception, describe it against what is claimed, remove it and restore the pattern — repeated until seeing through noise becomes a habit of mind. The scroll itself is the first contrast — the old phrase says black fire on white fire — and this build turns the tent to daylight: the asymmetry now stands as ink on parchment. The name on the door now says it plainly: the Twelve are ontological deceptions — lies about what a thing is — and the vows are vows of logic: to see truly, to speak truly, to act truly. The Gauge (footer) is your honest measure: Form A before training, Form B after a block; believe the between-form change, not the level counter.

The name and the vows

Cognition on its way to truth is mostly at war with its own noise — inconsistencies, errors, lapses: deceptions. Fluid intelligence, on this reading, is the capacity to see through that noise and re-organise cognition for higher agency. The Three Vows named here belong to the player; this game does not presume their content. In play they appear as three faces of one act — spot the deception (Vow I), describe it against what is claimed (Vow II), remove it and restore the pattern (Vow III). What Meru actually claims

Read at the source, Tenen's work rests on precise structures. The 27-letter alphabet is a 3×3×3 ternary matrix — each letter a triplet of 0/1/2 — and letters pair as reversed base-3 codes: Lamed (102) with Resh (201), He (011) with Mem (110), Bet (001) with Yod (100), the initial Bet with final Zadi (222 ≡ −001). The 28 letters of Genesis 1:1, strung as a bead chain, pair off completely only when the chain is wound at exactly 7 turns — pairs introduced in an AABA woven pattern. The first word splits into fire + thorn: an asymmetric vortex flame seated inside a perfectly symmetric tetrahedron ("the Light in the Meeting Tent"), whose silhouettes through each face yield the letter-forms — many appearances, one asymmetric gesture, modelled on the human hand. And B'limah, the untranslatable opening of Sefer Yetzirah, resolves as a pairing-key: without the key, the undivided 28-letter string admits hundreds of false parses.

The spotlight, the pointer, the fishing pole

Three further essences, read at the source. Tenen: "We aim the spotlight of awareness… We specify choice by direction. Our natural pointer is our hand" — the 27 letters as 27 pointing directions, a base space for attention itself. Here that is literal: the AIM prompts ask you to point attention down an axis (HANDEDNESS, NUMBER, THE VESSEL) and read off what it reveals; the veils train the same spotlight to exclude. His FAQ names the mechanism of study: the letter-sequence taken as "gestures in the mind… meditational exercises that enhance particular mental skills" — "not a fish… a fishing pole." HaLevi (Al Kuzari 4:25): “The shapes of the letters are not the result of accident, but of a device which is in harmony with the character of each letter” — form is function; and the Continuous Creation model “consists of exactly six model hands”, a discovered alignment with this game’s six gestures. The Rite enacts exactly this: chains of gestures performed on a seed purely in imagination, then unwound in reverse at the summit. The central graphic of the research is named at the source: Continuous Creation, a General Projective Principle — “all of the whole systems modeled represent a process of projection from one state into another”, one cycle of Singularity, Unfoldment, Wholeness: Seed → Tree → Fruit whose seed is inside itself. The Rite is that cycle enacted — forward from seed to fruit, inverse from fruit back to seed. And The Shadows exercise the principle at full strength: three threads projected by one generating gesture from different seeds, one thread cast by a stranger — the law must be induced across all three at once before the intruder shows. Why exactly three? The source answers: the Continuous Creation model “can be described with unusual and extraordinary elegance… by examining its 1-, 2-, and 3-dimensional symmetries” — three snapshots, one per dimension, “a unique identification”. Three true threads are the minimal complete testimony of one generator. On the 27 as pure ternary, that poster’s pairing is arithmetic: At-Bash is digit-wise complement, every digit summed with its partner making 2 — the alphabet’s mirror, distinct from the verse’s positional mirror. Both involutions are now playable in the Bead Chain, and the summit lie is answering one mirror with the other: Polarity Swap raised to the level of operations. His published poster of "At-Bash Meaning Pairs" shows the letters’ meanings themselves arranged as mirror-pairs — meaning reflected onto meaning. The polar trials enact this at second order: induce the witness pair’s law, reflect the law itself, and find its mirror in the field — a relation applied to a relation, with the witness’s own law planted as the trap. Read at the thread’s source, the scheme sharpens into exact correspondences. The Nine — All, Difference, Action, Division, Connection, Multiplication, Projection, Encompassment, Completion — mirror-pair end to end (All↔Completion, Difference↔Encompassment, Action↔Projection, Division↔Multiplication) with Connection alone at the center: the Woven Field renders this literally, four mirrored pairs about a lone spindle ✦. Inner vs Outer Multiplication is densify-the-unit vs duplicate-the-unit — exactly SWELL (size) vs GROW (count). Inner vs Outer Completion — the lower bound met vs the upper bound not exceeded — grounds the Frozen and Overrun Threads: a weave stopped short fails one bound, a weave run past fails the other. And the thread’s stated practice — juxtapose Inner, Outer, and Archetypal for everything contemplated — is the prompt ladder itself: named relations (Outer, the world’s form), aimed axes (Inner, the pointing self), induced and reflected laws (Archetypal, the law as such). Its relational-frame criteria — bidirectionality, combination, transformation — are trained respectively by the Bead Chain’s reversals, the Rite’s chains and unwindings, and the veils’ transfer across registers. The thread’s relational-frame criterion of combinatorial entailment — two relations combined yielding a third — is enacted by The Span: a witness chain climbs one step and then one step more, and the answer is the pair that composes both into a single two-step stride, against strides frozen at one, overrun to three, or collapsed to twins. Division and Multiplication, the mirrored archetypes of step and of gathering, meet here as composition. The matrix’s three levels (units·tens·hundreds as archetypal·inner·outer) ground the Scale-Echo: one law re-sounded at another register — a step in number answered by a step in size — which is analogical mapping across dimensions, a:b :: c:d with the dimension itself transposed. And the Bead Chain's code space is not decoration: 3×3×3 = 27, the full counting set itself, each bead a pure ternary pointer, paired as Tenen pairs the letters. And at the summit the mirror itself is counted, not glanced: a rite may carry three mirrors — net one — against the twin that pretends they cancelled — the Dirac String Trick made playable: as the source itself notes of the wine-dancer’s palm, “it also takes twice around… to return to its initial position”; “a rotation above and another rotation below the dancer’s shoulder — a double covering — untwists the dancer’s arm while the palm… turns continuously”. The forward and inverse rites are that winding and unwinding, played.

The mirrored Nine, the analytic and the synthetic

The thread’s own deepest structure: the nine archetypes mirror-pair from the ends inward — All↔Completion, Difference↔Encompassment, Action↔Projection, Division↔Multiplication — with Connection alone at the centre. The spindle ✦ of the Woven Field is exactly that lone centre, every cell answering its partner across it. The first four are named analytic functions, the last four synthetic; the seven windings walk the same arc — the analytic acts (spot the pair, the traitor, the lie) mirrored by the synthetic acts (enact the rite, complete the weave, echo the law) around the practice’s own centre. The thread further defines Inner and Outer Completion as lower and upper bound — nothing missing, nothing extra — which is precisely the Frozen Thread (stopped short of the bound) and the Overrun Thread (run past it) among the Twelve.

The archetype is induced, never shown

“None of the categories are observable without duality… the archetypal level represents the unitary aspect of two counterpoised categories, which cannot be observed directly, only through duality.” This is the exact doctrine of every witness trial: the law itself never appears — only its two faces do — and the player must induce the unobservable unity from them. The prompt ladder is the thread’s observer-triad made procedure: Outer (the relation named, or shown as a glyph), Inner (the spotlight aimed down an axis of one’s own attention), Archetypal (the law induced, reflected as its mirror-law, or re-sounded in another register). The thread’s recent proposal of a further “transcendent” layer, read rigorously, reduces to recursion on relations — relations taken as objects of relations — and that defensible kernel is implemented here exactly: polar induction (a law reflected), the Scale-Echo (a law transposed), One Gesture (rule-sets judged as wholes). The decorative remainder of that proposal is declined, on honesty.

Three · Seven · Twelve

The source ties these numbers to the Sefer Yetzirah’s own technical terms — T’li, Gal-Gal, Lav ≡ 3, 7, 12 — and to Young’s torus, whose surface is defined by a minimal seven-region map strung sequentially on one spiral: seven windings strung on one practice.

The whole practice keeps Sefer Yetzirah's partition that Tenen builds on — 3 mothers, 7 doubles, 12 elementals: three faces of the act (spot · describe · remove), seven windings of the trial, twelve canonical deceptions (echoing Arthur Young's twelve-fold geometry of meaning, which Tenen cites as the geometry of pointing). Your failure profile is kept against the Twelve, and from level 9 the game aims its forgeries at your weakest — the spotlight turned on the player.

The deception taxonomy

Every wrong option is a named lie drawn from how reasoning fails. Surface lure — shared colour and figure, broken structure. Twin-in-the-mirror — identical where mirrored was owed. Direction flip, label swap, polarity swap, off-by-one, negation veil — the five forgeries of testimony. Frozen / overrun / stranger thread — the classic matrix foils. Anagram bead — right digits, wrong order; off-code bead — reversed but one digit slipped. Noise veil — an attribute that varies meaninglessly while you try to attend past it. Errors are named on feedback: you learn your own failure profile.

The 27-fold Instructions Codex

The trial now has two inseparable movements. First, solve the live relation. Second, identify what kind of relation it was after the frame of reference is applied. A level is earned only when both structural sight and ontological classification are correct. This prevents category learning from collapsing into vocabulary rehearsal.

The matrix is nine operations across three frames. Archetypal names the relation itself; Inner asks how it appears within, toward, or upon the reference system; Outer asks how it appears outside, away from, or enacted by that system. The frame cue is part of the problem, not decoration. Levels 1–3 establish operations; levels 4–6 introduce inner framing; level 7 onward completes the outer register. Later levels remove verbal scaffolding, integrate several relations, increase interference, and preferentially revisit weak categories.

Why this is harder: every response requires relational induction, frame translation, category discrimination, and inhibition of close foils. Wrong-perspective foils preserve the operation but reverse the observer relation; paired-operation foils preserve the frame but change the underlying transformation. The 27-cell panel records both coverage and weakness so the generator can revisit what has not yet become fluent.

Why this can train fluid reasoning

Every trial demands fresh rule induction under engineered interference — nothing is template-recognisable across the generators' combinatorial space. Difficulty compounds along the g-loaded dimensions: relations integrated at once (to three simultaneous weave-rules and ordered triples), surface–structure conflict, negation, symbolic recoding and reversal, relation-of-relations (One Gesture), attentional veils, and time. The seven forms interleave within a single ladder, so each trial also demands task-set reconfiguration — and answers are machine-verified unique, so feedback is the one place deception never enters.

Honesty

Far transfer from any trained task to general fluid intelligence remains unproven. The Gauge exists for that reason: fixed, non-adaptive, feedback-free matrix forms. Run Form A before training and Form B after a block; trust the between-form change over any in-game level. If the Gauge doesn't move, the levels are skill, not transfer — worth knowing either way.
